NFC Gift

Digitally-personalised gifts for everybody

About the product

NFC Gift is a plushie with NFC tag, that allows you to add memories and wishes in for of photos and videos for your loved ones. It is secure and can be accessible only by scanning a NFC tag. To do so, you need to move your phone's back to the plushie's belly with NFC turned on. Content can be added by logging in with Google and adding your product to the account using code that was sent via email with your purchase.
